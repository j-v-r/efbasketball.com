About

 Elites Finest is a youth basketball program that has been in operation for over four years. Starting in 2012 with one team we now have five teams with over 65 players. We cater to kids 13 to 18 years old. Our program is open for kids throughout the Twin Cities area. Our teams play in tournaments in Minnesota as well as regional and national AAU basketball competitions. We have 10 coaches who dedicate their time and energy to ensure our teams and players are the best they can be. Our coaches have extensive experience in youth and high school sports; we work hard to ensure that the student athlete reaches their full potential both on and off the court.
